Empowering The Nation

Landscaping

Cost: R1 500

This program teaches the essentials of landscaping for developing new gardens and maintaining existing ones

what you will learn:

Garden design principles – planning layouts and selecting suitable plants. Planting techniques – preparing soil and planting for healthy growth. Pruning and trimming skills – maintaining the shape and health of plants. Watering and irrigation methods – ensuring gardens thrive sustainably. Garden maintenance practices – caring for both new and established gardens.
