Child Minding

Cost: R750

A beginner-friendly course on the fundamentals of child and infant care

What you will learn:

Basic hygiene and safety: How to keep infants clean, safely handle feeding equipment, and create a safe environment at home (covering things like safe sleep, choking hazards, and babyproofing). Feeding and nutrition: Understanding infant feeding options (breastfeeding, formula, and transitioning to solids), safe preparation of bottles, and age-appropriate portion sizes. Infant health and first aid: Recognizing signs of common illnesses, when to call a doctor, and basic first aid skills such as handling choking, fever, or minor injuries. Growth and development milestones: What to expect as babies grow—motor skills, language development, emotional bonding—and how to support healthy progress through play and interaction. Comforting and communication: Reading infant cues (crying, fussing, cooing), techniques for soothing (swaddling, rocking, routines), and building secure attachment.

Cooking

Cost: R750

Discover how to create simple, nutritious dishes the entire family will enjoy

What you will learn:

Balancing meals with key nutrients: How to combine proteins, carbohydrates, healthy fats, and vegetables in the right proportions to keep meals both satisfying and wholesome. Quick, budget-friendly cooking techniques: Ways to prepare meals using simple methods like one-pot cooking, roasting, or stir-frying that save both time and money. Adapting recipes for all ages: How to adjust spices, textures, and portion sizes so that children, adults, and even picky eaters can enjoy the same dish. Meal planning and prep skills: Strategies for organizing weekly menus, shopping smartly, and prepping ingredients ahead of time to reduce stress during busy days. Adding variety with simple twists: Creative ways to make classic family favorites healthier and more exciting—for example, swapping ingredients, adding hidden vegetables, or using herbs for flavor instead of extra salt.

Garden Maintenance

Cost: R750

Discover practical gardening techniques such as watering, pruning, and planting for a thriving domestic garden

What you will learn:

Smart watering methods: Understanding how often and how much to water different plants, plus techniques like deep watering and mulching to conserve moisture. Pruning for health and growth: How to trim plants correctly to remove dead or diseased parts, shape them for beauty, and encourage stronger flowering or fruiting. Soil preparation and planting basics: How to prepare soil, choose the right planting depth, and space plants correctly so they grow without competing for nutrients. Seasonal care and maintenance: What to plant during different seasons, when to fertilize, and how to protect plants from pests or harsh weather. Sustainable gardening practices: Simple eco-friendly habits like composting kitchen waste, companion planting, and reducing chemical use to keep your garden healthy long-term.
