Child Minding

Cost: R750

A beginner-friendly course on the fundamentals of child and infant care

What you will learn:

  • Basic hygiene and safetyKeeping infants clean, safely handling feeding equipment, and creating a safe home environment (safe sleep, choking hazards, babyproofing).
  • Feeding and nutritionBreastfeeding, formula, moving to solids, safe bottle prep, and age-appropriate portions.
  • Infant health & first aidSpotting common illnesses, when to call a doctor, and first aid for choking, fever, and minor injuries.
  • Growth & development milestonesWhat to expect as babies grow—motor skills, language, bonding—and how to support healthy progress through play.
  • Comforting & communicationReading cues and soothing techniques to build secure attachment.

Garden Maintenance

Cost: R750

Discover practical gardening techniques such as watering, pruning, and planting for a thriving domestic garden

What you will learn:

  • Smart watering methodsHow often and how much to water, deep watering, and mulching to retain moisture.
  • Pruning for health & growthRemove dead/diseased parts, shape plants, and encourage flowering or fruiting.
  • Soil prep & planting basicsPrepare soil, set correct planting depth, and space plants to avoid competition.
  • Seasonal care & maintenanceWhat to plant when, fertilising schedules, and protection from pests and weather.
  • Sustainable gardeningComposting, companion planting, and reducing chemicals for long-term garden health.
