Child Minding

Cost: R750

A beginner-friendly course on the fundamentals of child and infant care

What you will learn:

  • Basic hygiene and safety Keeping infants clean, safely handling feeding equipment, and creating a safe home environment (safe sleep, choking hazards, babyproofing).
  • Feeding and nutrition Breastfeeding, formula, moving to solids, safe bottle prep, and age-appropriate portions.
  • Infant health & first aid Spotting common illnesses, when to call a doctor, and first aid for choking, fever, and minor injuries.
  • Growth & development milestones What to expect as babies grow—motor skills, language, bonding—and how to support healthy progress through play.
  • Comforting & communication Reading cues (crying, fussing, cooing) and soothing techniques (swaddling, rocking, routines) to build secure attachment.

Cooking

Cost: R750

Discover how to create simple, nutritious dishes the entire family will enjoy

What you will learn:

  • Balance meals with key nutrients Combine proteins, carbohydrates, healthy fats, and vegetables in the right proportions.
  • Quick, budget-friendly techniques One-pot meals, roasting, and stir-frying to save time and money.
  • Adapt recipes for all ages Adjust spices, textures, and portions so kids and adults enjoy the same dish.
  • Meal planning & prep Plan weekly menus, shop smart, and prep ahead to reduce weekday stress.
  • Add variety with simple twists Healthier takes on classics—smart swaps, hidden veggies, and herbs for flavor.

Garden Maintenance

Cost: R750

Discover practical gardening techniques such as watering, pruning, and planting for a thriving domestic garden

What you will learn:

  • Smart watering methods How often and how much to water, deep watering, and mulching to retain moisture.
  • Pruning for health & growth Remove dead/diseased parts, shape plants, and encourage flowering or fruiting.
  • Soil prep & planting basics Prepare soil, set correct planting depth, and space plants to avoid competition.
  • Seasonal care & maintenance What to plant when, fertilising schedules, and protection from pests and weather.
  • Sustainable gardening Composting, companion planting, and reducing chemicals for long-term garden health.
